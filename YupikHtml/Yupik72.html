Peanuts

The humble peanut is a staple in classic cuisine, baking recipes and source of fiber. Peanuts make a great topper for salads, for ice cream, cakes and an essential ingredient for classics like peanut butter cookies and peanut brittle. Yupik peanuts are available salted and unsalted, shelled or unshelled, roasted or raw, whole, whole or in pieces, chocolate covered and BBQ seasoned!
