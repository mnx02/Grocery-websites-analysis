Almonds

Almonds are a good source of fiber and versatile nut that can be used as a topper for salads and yogurt, blended to make creamy almond butter, or added to baked goods like almond biscotti and almond tarts. Almonds are also a wholesome, natural snack that you can eat straight out of the bag. Yupik almonds are available organic, roasted or raw, shelled or unshelled, salted or unsalted, in flour or butter form, sliced, diced, slivered, blanched and organic.
